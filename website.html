About Me

I'm currently a sophomore attending the University of Delaware. I have experience coding in
C++, C, Java, Python, Typescript, HTML, and CSS. I also work at the university's IT support center
during the school year where I assist those who need help troubleshooting their software

I have participated in the annual hackathon hosted by my university where my team and I built

input they give on symptoms that aren't visible in an image such as being lightheaded. I have
also volunteered at many places including the Montgomery County Agricultural Fair and the City
of Rockville where I gained valuable experiences such as working as a team and communicating
with others effectively.

I am looking to be a software developer in the future with a strong interest in both frontend
and backend development. I find it interesting how I can tackle real world problems by
developing new software and I enjoy the process of designing the software. I will always be
on the lookout for chances to improve and learn new skills.
